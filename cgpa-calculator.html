CGPA Formula & Explanation

CGPA Formula

CGPA = Σ (Grade Point × Credit) ÷ Σ Credits

Example

If total grade points = 180

Total credits = 20

CGPA = 180 ÷ 20 = 9.0

Why CGPA is Important?

  • Used for academic performance evaluation
  • Required for scholarships & placements
  • Important for higher studies
