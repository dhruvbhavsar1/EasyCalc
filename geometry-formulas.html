Geometry Calculators

Geometry Formulas

Area of Square

Formula: Area = side × side

Area of Rectangle

Formula: Area = length × width

Area of Circle

Formula: Area = π × r²

Where π ≈ 3.14

Perimeter of Square

Formula: Perimeter = 4 × side

Perimeter of Rectangle

Formula: Perimeter = 2 × (length + width)

Circumference of Circle

Formula: Circumference = 2 × π × r

Where π ≈ 3.14

Area of Triangle

Formula: Area = ½ × base × height

Area of Parallelogram

Formula: Area = base × height

Area of Trapezium

Formula: Area = ½ × (a + b) × h

a and b are parallel sides

Diagonal of Rectangle

Formula: Diagonal = √(length² + width²)

Volume of Cube

Formula: Volume = side³

Volume of Cuboid

Formula: Volume = length × width × height

Volume of Sphere

Formula: Volume = ⁴⁄₃ × π × r³
